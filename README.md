# VCM Ecommerce Operations Analytics

A portfolio project created to demonstrate ecommerce operations, catalogue management, inventory tracking, and competitor analysis skills relevant to marketplace operations roles.

## Business Objective

To simulate day-to-day ecommerce operations activities for a spiritual products brand and create structured systems for:

- Product catalogue management
- Inventory monitoring
- Reorder planning
- Marketplace listing audit
- Competitor benchmarking

---

## Project 1: Product Master Catalogue

### Objective
Create a centralized product database for ecommerce operations.

### Skills Demonstrated
- SKU management
- Product cataloguing
- Listing readiness tracking
- Pricing management
- Margin tracking

### Deliverables
- Product Master Database
- Listing Checklist
- SEO Notes

---

## Project 2: Stock MIS Management

### Objective
Track inventory levels and identify reorder requirements.

### Skills Demonstrated
- Inventory management
- Stock monitoring
- Reorder planning
- Operations reporting

### Deliverables
- Daily Stock MIS
- Reorder Tracker

---

## Project 3: Competitor Listing Audit

### Objective
Analyze marketplace competitors and identify listing improvement opportunities.

### Skills Demonstrated
- Competitor research
- Marketplace analysis
- Listing optimization
- Opportunity identification

### Deliverables
- Competitor Audit Sheet
- Improvement Action Tracker

---

## Tools Used

- Microsoft Excel
- Data Validation
- Conditional Formatting
- Inventory Tracking
- Ecommerce Operations Analysis

---

## Outcome

Developed a complete ecommerce operations workflow covering catalogue management, stock control, and competitor analysis.
